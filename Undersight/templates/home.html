Frequently Asked Questions

Have any questions about Undersight?

  • How do I know reviews are legit?

    As with any other review system, it is not always to eliminate all fake or misinformed reviews. However, we have measures in place to prevent spam or repeated negative reviews by one user.

  • How can I trust this site?

    Our site relies on the support of the community. Thus, we also have to rely on the information that spreads within a community. While we work diligently to remove any incorrect information, we can't always factcheck every single review.

  • What types of politicians are included on this site?

    In addition to congress representatives, our sight currently supports mayors and and other local politicians. We are constantly updating our data base, so contact us below to have a politician in your area added in!
